I used Simon Marlow's lexer with very small modifications (https://github.com/simonmar/alex/master/examples/tiger.x)

I've benefitted from the following ML implementations:
     https://github.com/sunchao/tiger
     https://github.com/prikhi/modern-compiler-implementation-ml/
and the following Haskell implementations:
    https://github.com/svenpanne/tiger/
    https://github.com/unnohideyuki/Tiger-in-Haskell/

The LLVM codegen module is built closely following Stephen Diehl's
Kaleidoscope tutorial for llvm-hs.
